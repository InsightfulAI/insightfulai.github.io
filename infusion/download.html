Infusion is currently in beta development. You can join the testing program by following the instructions below.

Follow the iOS link and you will be prompted to download a beta version of Infusion. You will be prompted to download TestFlight, an Apple service for testing apps, and you will be able to install Infusion through TestFlight.

If you use Android, then simply follow the Android link and you will be asked to become a tester. Afterwards, you can install the app normally through Google Play.

If you're a journalist, please navigate to the "media" section and contact us instead by clicking here. We will provide you with another link.
