A Professional's Toolkit.

Infusion contains all the tools you'll ever need, and more. Packed with features, Infusion is designed to give you the most power over the creative process. Discover a new way to make art with the power of AI. Whether you are a designer, marketer or an artist, Infusion will contain the perfect features for you.

Stunning HD Artworks.

Powered by the state-of-the-art technology in neural style transfer, Infusion can make HD artworks up to 1080p while being fully offline. Its underlying algorithm makes no trade-off between speed, quality and versatility, allowing you to make amazing artworks quickly using any artwork as a style reference.

Fine-Tune Your Creations.

Infusion allows you to make fine changes so you can make the best possible AI artworks. For example, you can fine-tune how much stylisation is applied to the image so the final result will be more suited for your purposes.

A demonstration of adjusting the "stylisation amount" of an image. Smaller values resemble the original image more, whereas larger values are more stylised.

Prototype Rapidly.

With our exclusive Quick Preview render option, you can prototype your AI artworks much more quickly. The quick preview feature allows you to get a look into what the finished artwork will look like much more quickly, at the cost of only slightly reduced quality. This way, when you render the final artwork, you know it will look the best.

Left: Image rendered with Quick Preview on. Right: Image rendered with Quick Preview off, at the same resolution. The artwork rendered with Quick Preview was finished approximately 2.5 times more quickly.

Infinite Styles.

Infusion is not just limited to a handful of filters. In fact, it can paint artworks in any style as long as you provide the relevant style reference image. It can be van Gogh or Picasso, or a picture of a flame or a galaxy. Select any style that you see fit instead of having your creativity constrained by a handful of presets. There is also a Style Bank of preselected artworks to help you get started.

And More.

We are always adding new features to Infusion. These features may be available in the future: 4K artwork generation, batch image generation, adding your own images to the Style Bank, preserving colors and more. Stay updated via official Infusion news. We listen to your feedback. If you would like to suggest a feature, please leave a suggestion with the "feedback" button below.
