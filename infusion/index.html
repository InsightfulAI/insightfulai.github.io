Intelligent artwork creation.

Pick a photo. Pick a painting. Combine them. It's that easy.

You don't need a degree to make artworks in Infusion. You don't need to tell Infusion what colours to use. You don't need to adjust the width of the brush strokes or whatever. Like any good artist, Infusion can figure out what to do by itself.

Simply give it a photo and show it a painting, and Infusion will paint the photo in the painting's style. It's as easy as 1+1.

Avant-garde technology.

The most advanced AI to ever run on a phone.

Infusion is powered by a neural network - a form of artificial intelligence (AI) modelled after the human brain. This artificial intelligence is what gives Infusion its intelligence: its ability to recognise brush strokes and colours. It allows Infusion to perform a state-of-the-art technique called neural style transfer which creates beautiful artworks from pictures.

Confused by the technobabble? All you need to know is that Infusion is more advanced by anything that had come before. So advanced, that we had to write custom code to get it on your phone.
