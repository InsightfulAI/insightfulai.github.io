The hard numbers.

How many styles are there, anyway?

The whole point of Infusion is that it's an intelligent artist. As an intelligent artist, it is able to understand the features of any artwork and replicate them onto your photos. It works with any style: cubism, pencil sketches, impressionism and post-impressionism etc. You can even try some more interesting styles such as a mosaic or a picture of a fire.

Given that there are 1,592,524,800 possible images with the dimensions 1920x1080 (1920x1080x256x3), it could be said that there are approximately 1.59 billion possible styles that Infusion can use. However, that is not counting the various ways you can configure Infusion to produce different results, which results in an essentially infinite number of styles as you can simply run the output through Infusion again. On the other hand, it could also be argued that there are less than 1.59 billion styles as some of those pictures only differ by one pixel. There are certainly many different ways of counting, but the number of distinctive styles is so large that it may not be worth counting at all.
