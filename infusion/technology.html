Real AI. Real art.

Real intelligence. 34,276,299 neurons.

Infusion is based on the VGG19 convolutional neural network developed by a research team at Oxford. Its architecture mirrors that of a human brain and it was trained with huge image datasets. From those datasets, it extrapolated common image features which allows it to create artworks.

Infusion is based on the latest research in neural style transfer. The techniques used allows for the generation of high-quality artworks at an unprecedented speed. We worked hard to put this cutting-edge work into an app that you can use.

100% offline image generation.

Your images will never be uploaded to any server. Infusion does all of its work offline. That's right: if you download Infusion, then there really will be an intelligent artist that lives on your phone! Don't trust us? Turn off your internet and Infusion will still work.
